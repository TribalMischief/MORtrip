Interview Questions
Core Questions — All Subjects
  • Why are you in this business?
  • What's your philosophy?
  • What sets you apart?
  • Why do your clients work with you?
  • How does that relationship develop so your business becomes a partner in meeting their goals?
Leadership Track — Matt & Amy
  • Please describe MOR Wealth Management and how it is different.
  • Many firms talk about values, but you became B Corp certified. What motivated you to diverge from traditional wealth management?
  • How does your internal 'family' dynamic change how you handle high-pressure investment decisions or firm-wide growth?
  • How do you navigate the tension between maximizing financial returns and adhering to strict B Corp standards?
  • Wealth management is traditionally a 'black box.' How does your commitment to transparency impact how you communicate with your team and clients?
Client-Facing Team Track
  • When a client comes to you with a traditional portfolio, how do you walk them through aligning their wealth with their personal values without compromising long-term financial security?
  • B Corp status involves rigorous customer stewardship standards. How does this show up in the specific financial products or ESG strategies you recommend?
  • Beyond annual percentage return, what 'impact metrics' do you track? How do you show clients the tangible good their money is doing in the world?
  • Many clients feel overwhelmed by 'conscious' investing. How do you simplify these concepts to empower clients to make informed, mission-aligned decisions?
